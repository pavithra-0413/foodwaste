About Zero Waste Kitchen

Connecting surplus food with those in need

Our Mission

Zero Waste Kitchen is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste while helping to feed communities in need. We connect food donors (restaurants, caterers, supermarkets) with verified recipients (charities, shelters, community centers) to ensure good food gets to people instead of landfills.

How It Works

1. Donate

Food businesses register and post their surplus food with details about type, quantity, and pickup time.

2. Match

Our system matches donations with nearby recipient organizations based on needs and capacity.

3. Distribute

Recipients collect the food and distribute it to those in need, completing the cycle.
